# Risk Review Memo

## Core Questions

1. What are the main failure surfaces?
2. Who is harmed by each failure?
3. Which harms are likely, severe, or hidden?
4. What mitigations already exist?
5. Is human oversight genuinely effective?
6. What risks remain unacceptable?
7. What signal would force redesign, escalation, or rollback?
