# Ranking Review Memo

## Core Questions

1. What is the system optimizing?
2. What does that objective leave out?
3. Which secondary metrics guard against damage?
4. Who may be overexposed or underexposed?
5. Are offline gains likely to survive online?
6. Is exploration sufficient?
7. Should the team roll out, redesign, or keep testing?
