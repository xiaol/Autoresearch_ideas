# UniMatrix-Discovery (Combined)

![UniMatrix-Discovery](../assets/arch/umt_discovery.png)

**Mechanism.** Combine RuleMix, Timescale gating, ROSA memory, DeepEmbed modulation, and a spectral stability guard in a single Universal Transformer loop.

**Why it might help.** This is the “search space in one model” version: the network can route information through multiple mechanisms and discover which mixture yields the best scaling and long-context behavior.

**Tradeoffs.** Highest complexity and training cost; needs careful ablation and regularization to avoid brittle optimization.
