# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: Transfer Learning, Fine-Tuning, and Foundation Models
- Lesson goal: teach disciplined adaptation choices instead of tool-chasing
- Anchor case: enterprise support assistant
- Main skill or harness: ML Adaptation Decision
- Primary visual: prompt vs retrieval vs fine-tune decision path
- On-screen artifact: adaptation memo
- Viewer takeaway: the lightest method that can work should usually be tested first

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with a task that could be solved in many ways.
2. Separate knowledge problems from behavior problems.
3. Compare prompting, retrieval, PEFT, fine-tuning, and bespoke models.
4. Bring in latency, cost, grounding, and maintenance burden.
5. End with the Adaptation Decision harness.
