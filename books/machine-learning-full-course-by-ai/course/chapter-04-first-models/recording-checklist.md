# Recording Checklist

## Before Recording

- Re-run the delivery baseline script.
- Confirm the output still shows MAE `9.12` versus `1.94`.
- Open `assets/title-card.svg`.
- Open `assets/process-figure.svg`.
- Open `assets/evidence-panel.svg`.
- Keep `examples/delivery-time-prediction/artifacts/brief.md` ready for reference.

## During Recording

- Speak the problem before the model name.
- Do not rush the difference between naive and interpretable baselines.
- Keep the terminal zoomed enough to read the MAE values.
- Pause briefly on the evidence panel when explaining the result.
- Make the closing sentence explicit: complexity should earn its place.

## Visual Quality

- Title card is centered and legible.
- Terminal font is readable on a laptop-sized screen.
- No unnecessary windows appear during the demo.
- The process figure remains on screen long enough to understand the ladder.
- The evidence panel stays visible during result interpretation.

## Audio Quality

- Record a clean opening line twice.
- Avoid mouth noise and heavy keyboard sounds.
- Leave small pauses between major teaching beats for easier editing.
- Re-record the closing takeaway if the emphasis sounds weak.

## After Recording

- Confirm the demo command is visible at least once.
- Confirm both MAE values are visible in the export.
- Confirm the Baseline Builder prompt appears on screen.
- Confirm the final takeaway appears as text or narration.
- Watch the exported lesson once before using it as the course template.
