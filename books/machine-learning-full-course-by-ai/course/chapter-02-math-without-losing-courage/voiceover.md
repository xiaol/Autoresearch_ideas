# Voiceover Draft

## Opening

Most people are not blocked by machine learning because they are incapable of math. They are blocked because the symbols arrive faster than meaning does.

## Core Explanation

Vectors are structured examples. Matrices are many examples at once. Loss is a scalar measure of wrongness. Gradients tell us how the loss changes when the parameters move.

## Operational Turn

The key habit is not to worship notation. The key habit is to force every expression to answer four questions: what are the objects, what are their shapes, what operation is happening, and what does that look like in code.

## Skill Connection

That is why the ML Math Translator skill matters. It turns temporary clarity into a reusable translation system.

## Close

When a formula appears later in the course, your job is not to survive it. Your job is to translate it.
