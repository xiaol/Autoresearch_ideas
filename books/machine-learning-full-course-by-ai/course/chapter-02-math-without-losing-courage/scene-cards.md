# Scene Cards

- Purpose: open with the emotional barrier to ML math

- Narration beat: fear often comes from missing translation, not missing intelligence

## Scene 2

- Purpose: define the objects

- Narration beat: every symbol should have a job

## Scene 3

- Purpose: translate one update rule
- On screen: `w <- w - eta * grad_w L`
- Narration beat: parameter update is simply a direction-and-step story

## Scene 4

- Purpose: connect to code
- On screen: tiny code mapping
- Narration beat: formulas become less scary when they land in runnable logic

## Scene 5

- Purpose: introduce the harness
- On screen: ML Math Translator structure
