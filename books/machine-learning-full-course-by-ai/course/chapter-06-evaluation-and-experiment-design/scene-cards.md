# Scene Cards

## Scene 1

- Purpose: show a deceptively strong metric result
- On screen: high AUC with weak minority-slice performance
- Narration beat: good global numbers can still hide operational failure

## Scene 2

- Purpose: explain threshold and calibration
- On screen: score distribution and action threshold
- Narration beat: score quality matters when actions depend on it

## Scene 3

- Purpose: emphasize slice analysis
- On screen: region, segment, or merchant slice breakdown
- Narration beat: the average case can hide the painful case

## Scene 4

- Purpose: connect to experiment design
- On screen: one-change versus many-change experiment
- Narration beat: attribution determines whether we can trust the conclusion

## Scene 5

- Purpose: close with the harness
- On screen: evaluation review memo
- Narration beat: strong teams review evidence before they celebrate it
