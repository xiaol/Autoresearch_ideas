# Voiceover Draft

One of the biggest differences between beginner ML and professional ML is the ability to interpret results without flattering them.

Accuracy, AUC, precision, recall, threshold choice, calibration, and slice behavior all tell different parts of the story. If we collapse those into one victory number, we stop doing engineering and start doing theater.

The evaluation review harness exists to ask the uncomfortable question every strong team needs: what does this result still fail to prove?
