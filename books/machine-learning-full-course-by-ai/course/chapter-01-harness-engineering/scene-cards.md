# Scene Cards

## Scene 1

- Purpose: hook the viewer with the problem of unreliable AI-assisted learning
- On screen: chapter title plus a messy list of prompts, notes, and half-finished outputs
- Narration beat: speed is not the same as reliable progress

## Scene 2

- Purpose: define the four-layer ladder
- On screen: prompt -> workflow -> skill -> harness diagram
- Narration beat: each layer adds reuse and reliability

## Scene 3

- Purpose: make the concept concrete
- On screen: one example skill from the book, such as ML Math Translator
- Narration beat: a real skill shapes behavior under a task boundary

## Scene 4

- Purpose: connect the idea to professional growth
- On screen: learner, builder, engineer, specialist ladder
- Narration beat: better people do not only know more; they stabilize more of their process

## Scene 5

- Purpose: close the lesson
- On screen: one reflection prompt
- Narration beat: name one workflow you already use and ask whether it should become a reusable skill
