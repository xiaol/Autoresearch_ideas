# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: Optimization and Representation Learning
- Lesson goal: connect training behavior to model learning quality
- Anchor case: unstable neural training and document embeddings
- Main skill or harness: ML Training Diagnostics
- Primary visual: symptom -> ranked hypotheses -> next experiment
- On-screen artifact: diagnostics note
- Viewer takeaway: confusing loss curves should trigger structured diagnosis, not random changes

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with an unstable training run.
2. Separate optimization symptoms from data or label symptoms.
3. Explain representation learning at a practical level.
4. Show how to rank hypotheses and preserve attribution.
5. End with the Training Diagnostics harness.
