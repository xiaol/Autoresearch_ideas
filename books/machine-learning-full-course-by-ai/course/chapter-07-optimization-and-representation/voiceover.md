# Voiceover Draft

Training is where many learners become superstitious. The loss curve moves, validation behaves strangely, and suddenly every idea feels equally plausible.

The professional move is not to guess faster. It is to diagnose more clearly. We separate optimization problems from data problems, rank the likely causes, test cheap hypotheses first, and preserve attribution from one experiment to the next.

That is why training diagnostics is not just debugging. It is a way of thinking.
