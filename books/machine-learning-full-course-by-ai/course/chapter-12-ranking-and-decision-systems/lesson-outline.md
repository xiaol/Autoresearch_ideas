# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: Recommendation, Ranking, and Decision Systems
- Lesson goal: teach ranking as a feedback-driven system, not just a predictor
- Anchor case: recommendation feed
- Main skill or harness: ML Ranking Review
- Primary visual: objective -> exposure -> feedback loop
- On-screen artifact: ranking review memo
- Viewer takeaway: ranking systems change the data they later learn from

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with why recommenders feel powerful but distort behavior.
2. Explain objectives, candidate generation, and ranking.
3. Show the gap between offline gains and online reality.
4. Discuss exploration and exposure risks.
5. End with the Ranking Review harness.
