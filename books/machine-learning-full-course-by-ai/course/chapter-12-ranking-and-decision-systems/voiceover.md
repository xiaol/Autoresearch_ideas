# Voiceover Draft

Ranking systems are not just predictors. They are intervention systems. They shape what people see, what people click, and eventually what data the system receives back.

That is why a good ranking review asks more than whether one metric improved. It asks what the system is optimizing, what that leaves out, who gets overexposed or underexposed, and whether the online effects are likely to be healthy.

The ranking review harness pushes us to think like system designers.
