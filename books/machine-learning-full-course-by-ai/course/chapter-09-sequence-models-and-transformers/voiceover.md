# Voiceover Draft

Transformers become much easier to understand when we stop treating them as magic and start treating them as an answer to a specific pressure.

Sequence tasks force models to keep track of what matters from earlier context. Recurrent approaches do that through step-by-step state updates. Attention changes the routing logic by letting the model weigh relevance more directly across positions.

The architecture reading harness helps us turn that mechanism into engineering judgment instead of buzzword familiarity.
