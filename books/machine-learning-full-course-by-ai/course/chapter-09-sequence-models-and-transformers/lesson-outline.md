# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: Sequence Models, Attention, and Transformers
- Lesson goal: explain how sequence modeling evolved and why attention mattered
- Anchor case: support-conversation summarization
- Main skill or harness: ML Architecture Reader
- Primary visual: recurrent bottleneck versus attention routing
- On-screen artifact: architecture note

1. Open with why sequence tasks are hard.

3. Explain attention in plain language.
4. Connect the mechanism to transformer advantages and tradeoffs.
