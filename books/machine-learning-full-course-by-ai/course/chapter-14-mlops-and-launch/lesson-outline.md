# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: Training, Serving, and MLOps
- Lesson goal: connect trained models to operational reality
- Anchor case: support-ticket triage rollout
- Main skill or harness: ML Launch Readiness
- Primary visual: offline result -> serving -> monitoring -> rollback
- On-screen artifact: launch-readiness review
- Viewer takeaway: a model is not launch-ready until the service, monitoring, and rollback plan are real

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with a strong offline model that is still risky to launch.
2. Explain serving modes and operational stakes.
3. Show monitoring, drift, cost, and rollback requirements.
4. Walk through a launch review.
5. End with the Launch Readiness harness.
