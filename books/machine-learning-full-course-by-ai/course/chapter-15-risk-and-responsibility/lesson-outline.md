# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: Responsible AI, Safety, and Human Feedback
- Lesson goal: turn safety into concrete system review
- Anchor case: support assistant hallucination risk
- Main skill or harness: ML Risk Review
- Primary visual: failure surface -> harmed group -> mitigation -> residual risk
- On-screen artifact: risk review memo
- Viewer takeaway: responsibility becomes real when it changes workflow, monitoring, and escalation

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with a realistic harmful failure.
2. Map the system workflow and failure surfaces.
3. Show harmed groups, oversight quality, and hidden exposure.
4. Walk through the residual-risk review.
5. End with the Risk Review harness.
