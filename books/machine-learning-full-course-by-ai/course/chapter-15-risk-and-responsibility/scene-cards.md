# Scene Cards

## Scene 1

- Purpose: open with a concrete harmful failure
- On screen: assistant gives a wrong policy answer to a user
- Narration beat: safety begins in workflows, not slogans

## Scene 2

- Purpose: map the failure surface
- On screen: system workflow diagram with risk points
- Narration beat: risk becomes manageable only when it becomes visible

## Scene 3

- Purpose: identify harmed groups and hidden exposure
- On screen: failure -> harmed group matrix
- Narration beat: not all failures distribute their cost evenly

## Scene 4

- Purpose: inspect human review quality
- On screen: overloaded reviewer path
- Narration beat: claimed oversight is often weaker than real oversight

## Scene 5

- Purpose: close with the harness
- On screen: residual-risk memo
- Narration beat: safety must leave behind a document, not only an intention
