# Lesson Outline

- Lesson title: From Learner to Professional ML Engineer
- Lesson goal: make growth evidence-driven and visible
- Anchor case: a learner with several projects but unclear specialization direction
- Main skill or harness: ML Professional Growth
- Primary visual: strengths -> weaknesses -> artifact -> next review checkpoint
- On-screen artifact: development memo
- Viewer takeaway: careers grow through artifacts, review, and deliberate direction, not mood and buzzwords

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with the frustration of broad but shallow progress.
2. Review what visible artifacts reveal about real capability.
3. Show how to choose one growth direction.
4. Define one portfolio artifact and one review checkpoint.
5. End with the Professional Growth harness.
