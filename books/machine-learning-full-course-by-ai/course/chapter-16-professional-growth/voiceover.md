# Voiceover Draft

At some point, the question stops being whether you understand the chapter. The question becomes whether your work shows evidence of judgment.

A serious ML career grows through artifacts, review loops, communication habits, and deliberate specialization. The strongest learners are not only exposed to many ideas. They leave behind proof that they can build, evaluate, explain, and revise.

The professional growth harness turns career development into a visible system instead of a vague ambition.
