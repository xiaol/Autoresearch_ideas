# Bibliography

This is the canonical reference spine for the manuscript. Individual chapter `refs.md` files can point back here and add chapter-specific notes.

## Foundations

- Bishop, Christopher M. *Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning*. Springer, 2006.
- Murphy, Kevin P. *Probabilistic Machine Learning: An Introduction*. MIT Press, 2022.
- Hastie, Trevor, Robert Tibshirani, and Jerome Friedman. *The Elements of Statistical Learning*. Springer, 2009.
- James, Gareth, Daniela Witten, Trevor Hastie, and Robert Tibshirani. *An Introduction to Statistical Learning*. Springer.

## Deep Learning

- Goodfellow, Ian, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville. *Deep Learning*. MIT Press, 2016.
- Prince, Simon J. D. *Understanding Deep Learning*. MIT Press, 2023.
- Zhang, Aston, Zachary C. Lipton, Mu Li, and Alexander J. Smola. *Dive into Deep Learning*.

## Practice and Systems

- Huyen, Chip. *Designing Machine Learning Systems*. O'Reilly Media, 2022.
- Burkov, Andriy. *The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book*. 2019.
- Sculley, D., et al. "Hidden Technical Debt in Machine Learning Systems." 2015.

## Reinforcement Learning and Decision Making

- Sutton, Richard S., and Andrew G. Barto. *Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction*.

## Key Papers

- Kingma, Diederik P., and Jimmy Ba. "Adam: A Method for Stochastic Optimization." 2014.
- He, Kaiming, Xiangyu Zhang, Shaoqing Ren, and Jian Sun. "Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition." 2015.
- Vaswani, Ashish, et al. "Attention Is All You Need." 2017.
- Devlin, Jacob, Ming-Wei Chang, Kenton Lee, and Kristina Toutanova. "BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding." 2018.

## Editorial Reminder

When adding contemporary sources later, favor durable references over trend-chasing links. The goal of this book is to teach lasting structure, not only today's surface tooling.
